Kelly Gale showcases her insane abs in skimpy workout gear

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Victoria’s Secret supermodel Kelly Gale is known for her commitment to health and fitness. 

And the Swedish-Australian beauty showcased the fruits of her labour yet again on Tuesday after a gym session. 

Kelly highlighted her flat stomach and incredible abs as she posed in the mirror after sweating it out. 

Abs-solutely incredible: Victoria’s Secret model Kelly Gale (pictured) showcased her insane abs in skimpy workout gear after a sweat session in an Instagram photo on Tuesday

That star wore a plunging sports bra and a pair of bike shorts and wore her brunette tresses up and out of the way. 

The star had a surgical mask hanging off one of her ears and captioned the post with just a muscled arm emoji.  

It comes after Kelly denied over-exercising or starving herself to maintain a slender frame.

Denial:  It comes after Kelly denied over-exercising or starving herself to maintain a slender frame in a Q&A on Instagram earlier this month

During an Instagram Q&A, one fan asked the Victoria’s Secret model: ‘Did you ever over exercise and under eat?’  

‘Never I like to take care of my body and mind so I eat foods that are nourishing and taste good and I eat however much I want,’ Kelly responded.

‘I train to feel good as well and challenge myself. Lately I’ve been mostly excited about learning new martial art skills than any other training,’ Kelly added.

The question appeared to be in reference to recent scrutiny faced by Victoria’s Secret, who overhauled their business last year to be more inclusive, amid allegations they promoted unhealthy body image standards.  

In previous interviews, Kelly has spoken about walking 30km a day and enjoying boxing, skipping, running, resistance training and Pilates.

In 2017, she revealed her pre-runway training regimen included hitting the gym seven times a week for up to two-and-a-half hours at a time.

In an interview with Vogue Australia in 2018, the beauty said she consumes a balanced diet of lean protein, vegetables and healthy carbohydrates.   

‘I work out six days per week all year round and usually work out two hours per day, and on top of that I like power walking 15 to 30 kilometres each day,’ she told Vogue.

Kelly also relaunched her YouTube channel in 2020, where she breaks down her gym workouts into sessions focusing on upper body, abs, legs and booty.

Kelly first walked for Victoria’s Secret in 2013 when she was just 18 years old. She later appeared in the 2014, 2016, 2017 and 2018 shows.
