Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr. is an Indian actor, dancer, and a playback singer who works in Telugu films. He is best known for portraying the role of ‘Raja’ in the Telugu film “Yamadonga.” He is a recipient of two state Nandi Awards, two Filmfare Best Telugu Actor Awards, and four CineMAA Awards. He was ranked 27th in Forbes India Celebrity 100 list in 2012.

Career

Jr. NTR made his debut as a lead actor in 2001 with the Telugu film “Ninnu Choodalani.” Next, he appeared in V. V. Vinayak’s action drama “Aadi.” He played the role of an action hero in the film who takes revenge on a landlord for his parent’s death. The film performed well at the box office and was one of the highest-grossing films of the year 2002.

Subsequently, he featured in Telugu films like “Student No. 1,” “Simhadri,” “Rakhi,” “Yamadonga,” “Brindavanam,” “Baadshah,” “Nannaku Prematho,” “Janatha Garage,” and “Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava.”

Jr. NTR in a still from the movie Baadshah

In addition to acting, he is also a good singer. He has lent his voice to popular songs like “Lammi Thikkaregindha” from the Telugu film ‘Yamadonga’ (2007), “123 Nenoka Kantri” from the Telugu film ‘Kantri’ (2008), and “Geleya Geleya” from the Kannada film ‘Chakravyuha’ (2016).

Rao has endorsed various brands including Malabar Gold, Himani Navratna Hair Oil & talc powder, Boro Plus Powder, and Zandu Balm in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana.

In 2009, he campaigned for the Telugu Desam Party for the Indian General Elections.
