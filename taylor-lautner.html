Career

After relocating to California, he appeared in several minor television parts and commercials. Taylor Lautner starred in the film “The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl in 3-D” in 2005. He went on to star as Jack Spivey in the NBC drama “My Own Worst Enemy” in 2006. In 2010, he played Willy Harrington, Taylor Swift’s Valentine’s Day love interest in the Twilight film series. He also appeared in the films “Abduction” and “Breaking Dawn Part 2” in 2012 and “The Twilight Saga: Eclipse” in 2011 and 2012.
