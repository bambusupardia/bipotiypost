Kelly Ripa Agrees With Cher About Gray Hair: Not For Me

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Kelly Ripa is another celeb who will not be going gray.

While chatting with Ryan Seacrest on Live!, Kelly opened up about her graying roots as the show continues virtually amid the ongoing pandemic and said that the color gray is not for her.

Click inside to read more…

Kelly revealed to fans, and to Ryan, that she was attempting to hide some of her natural color.

“During the early days of the pandemic, I had embraced my gray hair privately while publicly I was using brown eyeshadow to paint my roots some shade that wasn’t Santa Clause white,” she shared. “But in real life, I go, ‘Oh, I look great with gray hair, you can barely tell,’” she joked while shaking her head ‘no.’

Kelly‘s comments follow Cher‘s, who revealed that she would also never go gray earlier this week.

“Cher was saying that gray hair is not for her. She is not caving in, she doesn’t care about the trend — you know, people are embracing their gray hair all over the place. Everybody’s embracing their gray hair,” Kelly added, before saying that she needs “to dye my hair.”

However, she doesn’t hate on any woman who decides to go gray, either.

“I think certain people look great with their gray hair,” she said. “I think it depends on the person. I really do.”
