T.I. Sets Omeretta Straight W/ Map Of Atlanta Post

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Omeretta upset half of Atlanta with the release of her “Sorry Not Sorry” track where she named several counties that she does not believe are part of Atlanta.

“College Park is not Atlanta /Lithonia is not Atlanta /Clayco is not Atlanta / Decatur is not Atlanta/ Gwinnett is not Atlanta /Roswell is not Atlanta /Forest park is not Atlanta /Lilburn is not Atlanta,” she rapped.

The song trended on Twitter:

“The craziest thing about that Omeretta song is that it’s managed to go this long without a single Atlanta rapper dropping a response ? I just KNEW n*ggas were gonna be quick with it,” one viewer wrote.

T.I., the self-proclaimed King of the South, disagrees. He used a legitimate map to correct her.

“Respectfully…. Only fools dispute facts. #ToWhomItMayConcern,” he captioned the post.

According to the map, Atlanta Airport is not in Atlanta. The airport is in College Park… which started it’s own debate in T.I’s comments.
