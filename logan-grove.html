Logan Grove Career

Logan Grove established his career primarily as a voice actor, famously known for portraying Gumball Watterson in The Amazing World of Gumball on Cartoon Network. His acting pursuits began at the tender age of 3 and expanded to include appearances on popular TV shows like The Suite Life of Zack & Cody, ER, and Medium. 

He also displayed his vocal skills as Garfield Logan in Young Justice and was featured in Angry Video Game Nerd: The Movie, a project helmed by YouTube sensation James Rolfe.
