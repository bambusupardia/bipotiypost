Publish date: 2024-08-14

Ian Alexander Jr., the son of actress/director Regina King, has passed away. And MTO News has confirmed that he’s suspected to have committed suicide.

Ian’s 26th birthday was Wednesday.

Ian was Regina”s only child, whom she shared with her ex-husband, record producer Ian Alexander Sr.
