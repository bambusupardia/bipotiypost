Billie Eilish's Hair Style Evolution Over the Years

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Billie Eilish has only been in the music scene for a few years now, but her incredible hits and her impeccable style has made her one of the biggest acts in the industry in a very short amount of time.

The 19-year-old Happier Than Ever superstar is known for her captivating visuals and vocal performances, but she’s also been a trendsetter as far as her personal style – especially when it comes to her ever-changing hair.

From different hues of light blue to black, to her signature “slime green” dye, to her formal debut as a blonde in 2021, Billie‘s provided a lot of excellent looks in just a short amount of time.
