Pichu's Apple Company by Keith Batcheller is the fourteenth book in the "Pokémon Junior" series, designed as a chapter book for beginners. It takes place in the Johto region. It was published by Scholastic and is 48 pages in length.

Blurb

After hearing of a band of starving Pichu that have been stealing apples, Ash and Pikachu decide to teach them how to protect themselves and the apple orchard before Team Rocket succeeds in stealing both the Pichu and the apples.
