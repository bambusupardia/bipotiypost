How many children does Travis Barker have and who are his kids' moms

Publish date: 2024-08-14

After being loved up for three years, Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker are expecting their first child together. 

The two have had a fairytale relationship with an extravagant engagement and wedding and are now adding a baby to the mix after a long IVF journey. 

The reality TV star announced her pregnancy at a Blink-182 concert in Los Angeles on June 16 while Travis was performing on stage. 

Gleaming with joy, she held up a sign which read ‘Travis I’m Pregnant’, the gesture recreated a scene from the band’s music video ‘All the Small Things’, during which a woman holds up the same sign. 

But how many children does the rock musician already have? Here is everything you need to know about Travis Barker’s children and their moms. 

Surprise! Kourtney Kardashian, 44, is expecting her first child with husband Travis Barker, 47 

Kourtney said that their doctor took them ‘down this road of doing IVF, and it hasn’t been the most amazing experience’

How many children does Travis Barker have?

Travis Barker has two biological children, with his ex-wife and American model, Shanna Moakler. 

His eldest child, Landon Barker was born in October 2003. The 19-year-old loves rock music just like his father and has realised music annually since 2018. 

His songs include ‘I Don’t Need Her’, ‘Trust’ and ‘Holiday’ and has even performed on stage drumming with Blink-182. 

He made headlines in June 2022 when he started dating the 19-year-old TikTok sensation and media personality Charli D’Amelio. 

The youngest member of the Barker family, Alabama, was literally born into the spotlight and featured in the MTV reality series Meet the Barkers. 

She pursued her love for music and uses TikTok as a platform, she has gained quite the following and has over 3 million followers. 

Born in December 2005, her father revealed that he daughter’s name was inspired by the character Alabama Whiteman in the movie True Romance- Travis’ favourite film.  

Travis also has a stepdaughter Atiana De La Hoya, the daughter of Shanna Moakler and boxing champ Oscar De La Hoya, who was five years old when her mother met her stepdad.

The 24-year-old artist and model shares a close relationship with her stepdad Barker, even after his divorce to Shanna in 2009.

Who are Travis Barker’s ex-wives?

The Blink-182 star has been married twice, but was only married to his first wife Melissa Kennedy for a year. 

Melissa now leads a much different life to Travis and stays away from the spotlight.  Dating Travis helped her break into the industry and she worked as the vice president of his clothing brand in 1999 until 2002. 

Melissa is said to be a real estate agent, having ventured into real estate after leaving Barker’s business- she has reportedly worked for different agencies and is now a broker. 

Ex: The drummer was married to Shanna until 2008

He married his second wife Shanna Moakler in 2004 during a gothic wedding ceremony, two years after his split from Melissa. 

Prior to meeting Kourtney Kardashian, Shanna was the most public relationship the rock star had. 

They even had a reality show about their lives called Meet the Barkers from 2005 to 2006. 

Their divorce was finalized in February 2008, but they continued to live together until 2012, saying it was in the best interest of their kids.
