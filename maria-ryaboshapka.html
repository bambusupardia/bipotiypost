Wiki/Biography

Maria Ryaboshapka was born on Saturday, 10 May 1997 (age 24 years; as of 2021) in Kyiv, Ukraine. Her zodiac sign is Taurus. She did her schooling at Kyiv’s School Number 23 (Школа № 23). In 2008, she joined Kyiv State Choreographic School (KSHU) to learn ballet dancing. In 2020, Maria pursued a course in performing arts (actor of theater and cinema) at Kyiv National I. K. Karpenko-Kary Theatre, Cinema and Television University. [1]

Career

Maria Ryaboshapka started her acting career in 2018 with the co-production film of Ukraine, Poland, Hungary, Lithuania, and Italy “Air.” The film was premiered worldwide in October 2018 during a program of the Rome International Film Festival.
