Blaziken

For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Blaziken.

Blaziken (Japanese: バシャーモEX BursyamoEX) is a Fire-type Basic Pokémon-EX card. It is one of the XY Black Star Promos.

Card text

Release information

The English print of this card was available in the English Hoenn Power Tins, released exclusively to Walmart stores on June 1, 2015, available elsewhere starting June 16, 2015. In Japan, this card was released as one of the XY-P Promotional cards available in the Rayquaza Mega Battle Mega Pack, released on April 17, 2015.
