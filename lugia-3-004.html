Release information

A framed copy of this tag and Ho-Oh was given away as part of a Mezastar Club Tag Present Campaign, which began on November 25, 2021. Three lotteries were held over the course of the campaign—eligibility for the first was open between November 25 and December 15, eligibility for the second was open between December 16 and January 5, 2022, and eligibility for the third was open between January 6 and 31, 2022. To become eligible to enter the lottery, Mezastar Club members were required to amass 100 points by registering dispensed tags from one set. Ten points were awarded for every tag registered and points could not be transferred upon the launch of a new Mezastar set. One hundred winners were selected in each of the prize draws for a total of 300 framed sets.[1]
