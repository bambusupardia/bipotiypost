Reset Stamp (Japanese: リセットスタンプ Reset Stamp) is an Item card. It was first released as part of the Unified Minds expansion.

Card text

Your opponent shuffles their hand into their deck and draws a card for each of their remaining Prize cards.
You may play as many Item cards as you like during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art Secret card in the Unified Minds expansion, first released in the Japanese GG End subset. Both prints feature artwork by sadaji. In Japan, the Regular print and a new print with artwork by OOYAMA were later included in the Tag All Stars subset. The Regular print was subsequently reprinted in the Charizard & Grimmsnarl VMAX Starter Sets, the Fighting Lucario V Deck, and a second version of the Charizard VMAX Starter Set from the VMAX Battle Triple Starter Set. The Tag All Stars print with OOYAMA's artwork was released in English as a Yellow A Alternate art card, available in the Trainer's Toolkit from June 26, 2020.

Trivia

This card has been banned from all sanctioned Pokémon TCG tournaments using the Expanded format since September 1, 2019 in Japan and November 15, 2019 internationally to promote a more enjoyable environment and change the format's reputation of disruptive strategies.
