(NECTA) THE NATIONAL EXAMINATIONS COUNCIL OF TANZANIA (NECTA) is a Government Institution that was established by the Parliamentary Act No. 21 of 1973. NECTA is responsible for the administration of all National Examinations in Tanzania.
