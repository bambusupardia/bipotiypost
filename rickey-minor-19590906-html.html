Rickey Minor is an American bassist, music director, composer and producer. He was the Music Director and Conductor of The Tonight Show with Jay Leno from 2010 to 2014, taking over after Kevin Eubanks left. Minor used to be a music director for American Idol. Minor has served as Music Director for many touring superstars including: Whitney Houston, Christina Aguilera, Ray Charles, Alicia Keys and Beyoncé and for television productions including: Grammy Awards, NAACP Image Awards and Super Bowl, for which he has received four Emmy nominations in the Music Direction category.
